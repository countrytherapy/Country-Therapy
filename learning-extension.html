Learning & Resources

Professional development, teacher consultation, and educational resources from Country Therapy.

Supporting Learning Through Occupational Therapy

At Country Therapy, we partner with schools, educators, and families to enhance learning outcomes. Our therapists provide evidence-based strategies and professional development focused on sensory processing, self-regulation, executive functioning, and inclusive education.

Key Areas We Support

  • Learning and Classroom Participation Assessments
  • Sensory Processing and Self-Regulation Support Plans
  • Attention, Concentration, and Executive Function Interventions
  • Fine and Gross Motor Development Programs
  • Behavioural and Emotional Regulation Strategies
  • Assistive Technology Recommendations for Learning Support
  • Environmental and Seating Assessments for Inclusive Learning
  • Teacher Consultation and Professional Development

Professional Development for Educators

We offer tailored workshops and training sessions for teachers and school staff on topics such as sensory integration in the classroom, supporting neurodiverse students, and practical strategies for inclusion.

Contact us to discuss how we can support your school's learning environment.
